from my family trip to china and hong kong back in 2019. just a lot of colors and memories. recently, i think i've started to be lazy with picking up a camera and snapping away at moments, but i feel grateful now that i did so on this trip because while the memories that we are enjoying at moments do not last forever, the photographs we take are eternal. i don't really intentionally try to make my photography colorful, but i think it always ends up that way. we are so lucky to be living in such a colorful world. joy!
